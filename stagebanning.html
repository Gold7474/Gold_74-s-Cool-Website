Stage Banning

Banning procedure:

Game 1:
Higher seed bans 1 stage, lower seed picks 1 of the remaining stages.

Game 2:
Winner of Game 1 bans 2 stages, loser of Game 1 picks 1 of the remaining stages.

Game 3:
Winner of Game 2 bans 2 stages, loser of Game 2 picks 1 of the remaining stages.

Game 4 (Grand Finals Only):
Winner of Game 3 bans 2 stages, loser of Game 3 picks 1 of the remaining stages.

Game 5 (Grand Finals Only):
Winner of Game 4 bans 2 stages, loser of Game 4 picks 1 of the remaining stages.

OR

If both contestants agree, a random stage can be chosen.
PLEASE NOTE WHEN SELECTING A STAGE:
When choosing a stage, a player cannot select a stage that they have already won on during the set.

Legal stages:
  • Battlefield
  • Final Destination
  • Fountain of Dreams
  • Pokemon Stadium
  • Yoshi's Story
  • Dream Land
